About Us

Established 15 years ago as an independent property developer, Senol has steadily grown over the past decade, developing larger projects for a variety of niche and mainstream investors.

We are a company with big plans, delivering quality residential and commercial properties for the Melbourne market with intentions to expand internationally.

At the helm, CEO Rustu Senol imparts his experience, expertise and natural intuition for market trends, ensuring we remain one-step ahead of the game in a constantly evolving property market. This strategy and approach along with our commitment to putting the needs of owners first has helped us grow from humble beginnings to where we are today.

We provide our clients and referral partners with quick and easy access to comprehensive developments in key growth areas, offering options that cut across the full spectrum of commercial and residential property developments, including land subdivisions and townhouses. Offering true turnkey solutions designed to provide optimum returns for the client, we pride ourselves on delivering the best value possible and ensuring our properties maintain a high-standard of excellence and quality finishes.
